Artist Statement

Ever since I was a child I have been fascinated by the beauty
of nature. What starts out as something that seems
so obvious as the earth, has such a deeper meaning and
connection.

to ourselves as human being – just one type of being and
inhabitant on this beautiful planeMy work explores the
relationship and connection with ourselves
and nature.
